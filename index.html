Crystal Collector Game

How to Play!

You will be shown a random number at the start of each game.

There are four crystals below. When you click on a crystal images, it will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win if your total scores matches the random number.

You will loss if your scores goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal images is hidden from you until you click on crystals.

The game will restarts whenever you win or loss.
